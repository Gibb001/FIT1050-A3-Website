Ducks

At Quack It, we’re proud to offer healthy, well-raised Muscovy ducklings, ducks, and drakes to hobbyists, breeders, and backyard enthusiasts. Whether you’re starting a flock, expanding a breeding program, or looking for show-quality birds, our ducks are selected for their temperament, conformation, and vitality.

Click to show more

In addition to top-quality birds, we provide expert breeding consultations and reliable care advice to help you succeed. Our team is passionate about ducks and all animals, and we're dedicated to ensuring each customer has the knowledge and resources they need to raise healthy, thriving Muscovies. Whether you're a first time duck owner or an experienced breeder, we're here to support you every step of the way. Muscovies are quiet, hardy, and easy to care for. They adapt well to a variety of climates and are known for their friendly nature and strong foraging instincts. They are great for pest control, companionship, or exhibition. Their calm temperament makes them ideal for families and hobby farms alike. Every purchase includes personalized guidance on feeding, housing, and behavior, so you can feel confident from day one. We’re committed to your success and offer ongoing support as your flock grows. From selecting the right setup to addressing common challenges, we’re here to ensure your experience with Muscovies is both rewarding and enjoyable.

Background

Quack IT began in late 2013 as a small, family-run business with a mission to support our rural community's tech needs. Since then, we’ve expanded to provide reliable solutions for everything from PC parts to computer repairs and troubleshooting. Whether you’re building a custom setup or just need help with everyday issues, we’re here to make technology work for you. In addition to our tech services, here at Quack IT we have a love and passion for breeding and selling ducks. What started as a backyard hobby has become a beloved part of our business. We raise healthy, well-cared-for ducks and offer them to local families and small farms. It’s a fun and meaningful way we stay connected to our agricultural roots, combining tech support with a touch of country charm.
